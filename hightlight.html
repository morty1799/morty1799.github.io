Motion Graphics

This project involves collaborating with an independent musician from Hong Kong, AnnaHisbbuR. It marks my initial foray into creating motion graphics using Photoshop and Premier Pro, with a production timeline of approximately four weeks. The video's concept aligns well with the song's theme, focusing on a young girl's struggle with depression and her effort to connect with others facing similar challenges to foster a sense of unity. The video's main objective is to effectively communicate the song's message while also aiming to create a sleek and minimalistic look for both the music and its visuals.
